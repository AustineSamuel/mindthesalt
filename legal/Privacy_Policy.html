Privacy Policy



Mind the Salt is dedicated to safeguarding your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled responsibly and securely. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect the information you provide when using our website, services, and Mind the Salt Game App.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, and preferences when you subscribe to our newsletter or register an account. Responses to surveys, feedback forms, or participation in challenges.
  • Non-Personal Information: Device details (e.g., browser type, operating system) and usage data, such as pages visited and time spent on the site.
  • Cookies and Tracking Technologies: Cookies enhance your browsing experience and are used for analytics, personalisation, and improving site functionality.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Improving User Experience: Tailored recommendations, updates, and enhanced website and app features.
  • Communication: Sending newsletters, updates, educational materials, and responding to inquiries or requests.
  • Compliance and Safety: Ensuring legal obligations are met and fraudulent activities are prevented.

3. Sharing Your Information

Your information will never be sold or rented to third parties. However, it may be shared with trusted service providers who assist us in operating the website and game app, such as:

  • Email service providers for newsletters.
  • Analytics providers (e.g., Google Analytics) to improve performance.

These partners adhere to strict data protection policies.

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

5. Your Rights

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal information.
  • Correction: Request corrections to inaccurate information.
  • Deletion: Request that we delete your personal data, where applicable.
  • Opt-Out: Opt-out of newsletters or marketing emails at any time.

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@mindthesalt.com.

6. Security Measures

We use industry-standard security measures, including encryption and secure servers, to protect your data. However, no online platform is entirely secure, and we cannot guarantee complete protection against breaches.
