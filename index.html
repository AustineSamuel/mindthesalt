About Us

Mind the Salt is a health education initiative dedicated to helping communities—especially those of African descent—understand the impact of salt and how to enjoy meals with less sodium. We provide practical tips, culturally relevant advice, and fun activities to empower families to take charge of their health.

Our Vision

We imagine a world where everyone can savour the natural flavours of their foods while keeping their hearts and bodies strong. By embracing knowledge and making small changes, communities can enjoy balanced meals and healthier futures.

Our Mission

We strive to deliver accessible, culturally sensitive information on salt reduction. Through interactive tools, engaging stories, and actionable advice, we inspire individuals and families to reduce salt, embrace fresh ingredients, and protect their health.

Part of a PhD Project

This initiative stems from the doctoral research of Jesse Enebi Usman at Sheffield Hallam University. Supported by the university, Jesse’s work focuses on improving health outcomes through education, ensuring underserved communities have the resources to make better dietary choices. Your journey to lower salt is part of a broader mission to enhance equity in healthcare.

The Truth About Salt

Salt influences your heart, brain, and kidneys. Understanding its effects helps you manage your intake. Mind the Salt guides you in using herbs, spices, and citrus for bold flavours without reliance on salt. Knowledge is power—let’s embrace a healthier, more vibrant way of eating.
